Privacy Policy for Marketplace Assistant

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Marketplace Assistant processes information from Facebook Marketplace only when the user explicitly starts a listing scan, copy, or draft operation.

Information processed

The extension may process listing titles, prices, vehicle attributes, descriptions, listing URLs, photographs, workflow status, interface language, and technical error messages related to the current operation.

How information is used

This information is used solely to copy a listing selected by the user and prepare a new Marketplace draft for the user to review. Marketplace Assistant does not automatically publish listings or send messages.

Storage and transfer

Listing information and photographs are stored locally in Chrome extension storage or temporary files on the user's device. The current version does not transmit listing content, Facebook credentials, browsing history, or photographs to the developer's servers. Temporary files created for photo upload are removed after the operation where possible. Locally stored workflow information can be removed by clearing the extension data or uninstalling the extension.

Authentication information

Marketplace Assistant does not request, collect, or store Facebook passwords. It operates within the Facebook session that the user has already opened in Chrome.

Sharing and sale of data

The developer does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties and does not use it for advertising, creditworthiness, or profiling.

Security

The extension limits its operation to Facebook Marketplace and Facebook-hosted image domains. Users should install Marketplace Assistant only from the official distribution link provided by the developer.

Third-party services

Facebook Marketplace is operated by Meta. Use of Facebook is subject to Meta's own terms and privacy policies. Marketplace Assistant is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Meta or Facebook.
